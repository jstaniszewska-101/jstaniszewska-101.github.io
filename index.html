About

I've spent years building businesses — and slowly discovered that the most important transformations begin from within. Today, as co-managing director of Being in Systems, I work as a transformation servant: creating spaces where leaders and organizations can meet themselves differently.

With 20+ years of experience across 20+ international markets, I work as an independent consultant with organizations across the European Union and the United States — offering facilitation, leadership program design, and systemic advisory rooted in Theory U, Teal organizations, and Emergent Entrepreneurship.

What I do

Facilitation

Sessions and programs for executive teams navigating complexity. I work in the space where organizational structure meets human awareness — 2+1 individual format and group curriculum programs.

Consulting

Available for contract engagements across the EU and USA — organizational advisory, leadership program design, and systemic change processes.

Writing

Author of a philosophical guide to organizations — mapping the interior conditions that shape collective life, for leaders who want to see whole.

Education

MBA in Creative Enterprises, Meridian University CA. Exploring the intersection of creativity, leadership, and systemic transformation.
